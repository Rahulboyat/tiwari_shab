VIRTUAL REALITY PLAN

Interior design

With VR, customers can virtually walk through and experience a fully-realized, immersive interior design plan. This can include realistic textures, colors, and lighting that can help clients visualize how the final product will look.

Exterior Rendering

VR technology can also be used to create realistic, immersive 3D models of the exterior of a building, allowing clients to explore and experience the look and feel of the building from different angles and viewpoints.

Point view

In a Point View VR experience, clients can view specific design elements and features up close and in detail. This could include furniture, fixtures, and other design elements that might be difficult to visualize through traditional 20 designs.

Walk through

VR allows clients to virtually walk through and explore the full layout of a building, experiencing the space from different angles and viewpoints. This can help clients get a better sense of how the space will look and feel, and can help identify any potential issues or concerns before construction begins.

BASE PLAN

Floor

A floor plan is a scale drawing that shows the layout of a building or structure from above. It includes details such as room sizes, wall thicknesses, and door and window locations. Our team of experienced architects will work with you to create a floor plan that maximizes your space and meets your functional and aesthetic goals

Side Elevation

A side elevation is a drawing that shows the vertical view of a building or structure from one side. It includes details such as the heights of walls, the location of doors and windows, and the slope of the roof. Our team will create a side elevation that matches the style and character of your building while ensuring that it meets all necessary codes and regulations.

Front elevation

A front elevation is a drawing that shows the vertical view of a building or structure from the front. It includes details such as the facade, the placement of doors and windows, and any decorative features. Our team will create a front elevation that is visually appealing and complements the overall design of your building.

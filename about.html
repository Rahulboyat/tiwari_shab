About Us

House of Dreams is a dedicated affordable housing planning company specializing in custom solutions for the middle class. We address common homeowner issues like sunlight, ventilation, stability, and durability. Through in-depth site analysis, we create tailored plans. Our services include 3D models and virtual reality experiences for clients to visualize their dream homes. We're committed to transforming housing dreams into reality.
